Our Quarries

Our unique and beautiful decorative natural stone quarries are some of the finest to be found anywhere in the world and offer consistent, high quality natural stone in quantities to supply our customers for generations, rather than just years.

We operate our quarries in a way that provides our employees with safety and comfort. Along with safety our top priorities are care of the land and conservation of our resources. Our efforts have benefited a variety of local wildlife and have protected large scenic areas and geological study areas over a billion years old.

You can learn more about our quarries at Northern Stone Supply.
